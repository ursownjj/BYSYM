Our Story

Let us introduce our self as one of the leading contractors handling mechanical jobs in our industrial sector since the last 13 years. Our field of operations includes Erection, Alignment and Commissioning of Rotary Equipment's including refurbishing, Fabrication, Erection and Piping, Production Maintenance and Shutdown jobs related with Steel plants, Power Plants etc. Established in 2004, BYSYM CONSTRUCTION is engaged in Erection, Alignment, Testing and Commissioning Rotary Equipments together with Structural Fabrication and Erection and All Kinds of Piping etc., connected with different industrial projects in Karnataka and also in maintenance of running plants, production and shut down jobs.
